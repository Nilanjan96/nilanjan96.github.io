Some of my favorite albums are-

Reanimation by Linkin Park

Reanimation was one of the first albums that I ever listened to. Growing up as an avid Linkin Park fan, some of my other favorite Linkin Park albums include Minutes to Midnight and Meteora.

Random Access Memories by Daft Punk

The music of Daft Punk has always been mysteriously amazing to hear to. With their repetitive beats and funky base, Random Access Memories brought together some of the greatest in the industry together to make one of the best disco albums ever made.

Phobia by Breaking Benjamin

Phobia is one of my favorite rock albums ever. The slow start from Intro to picking up the pace in the amazing Diary of Jane, Phobia as an album, talks about different fears that a person faces in his life.
